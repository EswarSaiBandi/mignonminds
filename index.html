FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does waterproofing work take?

Bathroom waterproofing typically takes 2-3 days, while terrace waterproofing can take 5-7 days depending on area size. We provide exact timelines after inspection.

What is the warranty period?

We offer up to 5 years warranty on waterproofing work and 6 months on interior work. Warranty terms are clearly documented in the agreement.

Do you serve areas outside Hyderabad?

Yes, we serve all areas in Hyderabad and nearby Telangana districts. Contact us to check service availability for your location.

What is included in the free inspection?

Our expert visits your site, diagnoses the issue, measures the area, and provides a detailed quote with material specifications—completely free.

How much does interior work cost?

Interior costs range from ₹1,200-2,200 per sq ft depending on the package and materials chosen. We provide transparent BOQ before starting work.

How quickly can you respond to my inquiry?

We respond within 24 hours to all inquiries via phone, WhatsApp, or email. Urgent requests are prioritized for same-day response.
