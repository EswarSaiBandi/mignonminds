VII — Inquiry

Frequently asked.

How long does waterproofing work take? +

Bathroom waterproofing typically resolves in 2–3 days; terrace waterproofing in 5–7 days, depending on area. We provide a precise timeline after the inspection.

What is the warranty period? +

Up to 5 years on waterproofing and 6 months on interior works. Warranty terms are documented clearly within the agreement.

Do you serve areas outside Hyderabad? +

Yes — across Hyderabad and adjoining Telangana districts. Reach out and we will confirm coverage for your location.

What is included in the free inspection? +

An expert site visit, on-site diagnosis, area measurement, and a detailed quote with material specifications — at no cost.

How is interior pricing structured? +

Interior packages range from ₹1,200 to ₹2,200 per sq ft, depending on materials and finishes. A transparent BOQ is shared before execution begins.

How quickly can you respond? +

We respond within 24 hours via phone, WhatsApp, or email. Urgent enquiries are prioritised for same-day attention.
